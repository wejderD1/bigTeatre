Bolshoi Theater - St. Petersburg theater, which existed in 1784-1886, since 1886 - Petersburg Conservatory. The first permanent in St. Petersburg, the largest in Russia and one of the largest theater buildings in Europe XVIII.

abouttheatre

The best theater in St. Petersburg

According to archival documents, the first stone building of the Bolshoi Theater began to be erected in 1775 according to the project of Antonio Rinaldi. Later, after Rinaldi fell from the woods and could not personally observe the progress of work, Catherine II commissioned the German theater decorator and architect Ludwig Philip Tischbein to create a new theater project, which was embodied by architects F.W. von Baur and M.A. Dedenev. It was opened in 1783, although, based on other evidence, 1784 can be considered the year of its opening.
